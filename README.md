## About the Project

An end-to-end data analysis project completed during an internship at **SkillCraft Technology**. The task involved cleaning a raw Superstore sales dataset, building Pivot Tables and Charts for three key business conditions, and delivering the findings through an interactive Excel Dashboard.

---

## Task Requirements

Three visualizations were required as part of the assignment:

| # | Visualization | Objective |
|---|--------------|-----------|
| 1 | Total Sales by Category | Identify top revenue-generating product categories |
| 2 | Total Profit by Category | Assess profitability and detect loss-making categories |
| 3 | Sales Trend Over Time | Track how sales have progressed across time periods |

---

## Data Cleaning

The raw dataset was cleaned in Microsoft Excel before analysis. Steps performed:

- Removed duplicate records
- Handled missing and null values
- Standardized date formats
- Fixed inconsistent category and region labels
- Validated data types across all columns

---

## Analysis & Visualizations

Each visualization is saved as an independent Excel file with its own dedicated Pivot Table and Chart.

**Total Sales by Category:-** 

Column chart comparing total revenue across all product categories, making it easy to identify the highest and lowest performing segments.

**Total Profit by Category:-**

Chart displaying net profit per category. Conditional Formatting was applied to distinguish positive profit (green) from losses (red) at a glance.

**Sales Trend Over Time:-**

Line chart tracking sales progression over time, surfacing seasonal patterns and overall business growth trends.

---

## Dashboard

All three visualizations are brought together in a single Excel Dashboard — structured, clean, and designed to give a full business overview without navigating multiple files.

---

## Conditional Formatting

Applied exclusively to the **Profit by Category** section due to its mix of positive and negative values. Green highlights profitable categories while red flags loss-making ones, enabling faster interpretation without reading through raw numbers.
